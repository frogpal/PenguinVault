### Ingredients

**Dry Ingredients:**
- 1¾ cups (210g) spelt flour (or whole wheat, all-purpose, or gluten-free blend)
- ⅓ cup (75g) sugar (or ½ cup finely chopped dates)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

**Wet Ingredients:**
- 3-4 overripe bananas (1½-1¾ cups/338-410g), mashed
- ⅓ cup (75ml) neutral oil (or coconut oil, vegan butter, or applesauce)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ¼ cup (56ml) almond milk (if needed)

**Optional Add-ins:**
- 59-78g chopped walnuts
- ¼-½ cup chocolate chips
### Instructions

- Preheat oven to 180°C and grease loaf pan
- Mash 3-4 bananas (1½ to 1¾ cups) in a medium bowl
- Add oil/applesauce and vanilla extract, mix well
- Add flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt; mix until just combined (don't overmix, some lumps are fine)
- Add milk if batter seems too thick, especially with whole grain flours
- Pour batter into greased loaf pan
- Bake 50 minutes to 1 hour (test with toothpick in center—should come out clean)
- Cool 10 minutes before slicing
- Store covered at room temperature for 3 days, refrigerated for 1 week, or frozen for 2 months
