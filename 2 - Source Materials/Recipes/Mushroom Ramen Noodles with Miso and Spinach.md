### Ingredients

**Aromatics & Oil:**
- 2 tbsp coconut oil (unrefined) or olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, microplaned or finely grated
- 1 tbsp fresh garlic, microplaned or finely grated
- 4 green onions, sliced thin (whites and greens separated)

**Main Ingredients:**
- 340g shiitake mushrooms, stems removed, sliced thin
- 2 bundles (152g) soba noodles (GF if needed)
- 5 cups (95g) baby spinach

**Liquids & Seasonings:**
- 4 cups (940g) vegetable broth
- 3 cups (675g) water
- 2 tbsp white miso paste
- Tamari to taste
- Sriracha to taste

**For Serving:**
- Sesame seeds
- Sriracha
- Tamari
- Green onions
- Cilantro
### Instructions
1. Heat oil in pot. Add mushrooms, ginger, garlic, and white onion parts. Cook for 6 minutes, stirring occasionally, letting mushrooms sear on bottom.
2. Add broth and water, bring to boil. Reserve 1 cup broth, then add noodles and simmer 5 minutes until tender.
3. Whisk miso paste into reserved broth, pour back into pot. Add sriracha and tamari to taste.
4. Serve topped with spinach and sesame seeds. Store leftovers up to 3 days refrigerated.
